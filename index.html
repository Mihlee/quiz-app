Everything you need to know about DevQuiz

DevQuiz was designed as an open-access gamified sandbox to bridge the gap between technical theory and fast-paced assessment recall. Whether you're refreshing your knowledge before a technical screen or reinforcing daily coding lessons, this application covers foundational rules across the entire frontend engineering spectrum.
